Corona Beana

Description

I like to cook the Coronas up in a can of diced tomatoes, a can of tomato sauce, a baseball or larger diced onion, a diced carrot, a diced celery spine, about 1/8 cup minced garlic, a bit of thyme, a bit of tarragon, a bit of Aleppo pepper or red pepper flakes, a couple tablespoons of Bragg Liquid Aminos, fresh-cracked pepper, and 4 cups of water (per 1-pound bag of beans). I let them pressure cook for an hour and 10 minutes (though a newer cooker may take less time). I open the pot up and test them; if they are still crunchy I close it up and run them for another 10-15 minutes. After the beans are done, I switch the pot to saut√© or simmer, and reduce the liquor until it is nice and thick. When eating, a sprinkle of lemon juice and fresh parsley is great on these, but they aren't necessary.
